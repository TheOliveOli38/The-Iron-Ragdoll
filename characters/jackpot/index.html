Biography

Jackpot got extremely rich after inventing some sort of contraception pill with absolutely no side effects for anyone. He had always wanted to expand his market, especially to Moreland where "they breed like rabbits," but as you can guess the Moose aren't that fond of someone who compares them to rabbits.

Until one day he met a certain three dumb kids with a Sylvandian girl, wanting to pass through Atlasia to Moreland.
