Sparky

Design inspiration: Diesel (from Warriors: Graystripe's Adventure)

Character inspiration: Pippi Longstockings.

She's the main character, duh. Her design was originally a cat named "Lightningtail" (before I knew about that ThunderClan deputy) who was inspired by Diesel, as mentioned above. Later I made her into an indigoblood troll, and renamed her "Sparky" to fit the six-letter formula of Homestuck trolls. Needless to say, I like this name better.

Icicle

His legacy name was Falcon Icicle, a goldblood troll. Now I deleted the "Falcon" and made him Icicle instead.

Cosmo

Inspiration: Ceruleanbloods (Homestuck).

Her legacy name was Cosmic, a compromise for the six-letter formula. And I didn't come up with the different-colored eyes until pretty late, but I'm glad for the choice. The insides of her ears are actually gradient, but most of the time you only see a little bit of that.

Tomato

Inspiration: Rustbloods (Homestuck).

He is Potato's twin. Yes, bad name choices!

Useless trivia: Tomato and Potato's legacy troll names had the same first names and last names (Tomato Tomato and Potato Potato). However, the names were pronounced differently: to-MAY-to to-MAH-to.

Potato

Tomato and Potato only became twins after I made this comic original, you know, since trolls with different blood castes obviously can't be twins, but they were such a great pair of twins. One talks fast and the other slow, but both are down-to-earth and practical.

Melody

Design inspiration: badass trench coats.

Wears a badass trench coat. Those coats are always badass, try to change my mind.

I spent ages learning how to draw that coat. In the end, drawing it felt so simple that I wondered why it even took me that long to learn. I guess this holds true for everything you learn.
