# Lotus Lake Temple / 莲花湖寺

The most famous temple in [Fanton](/world/fanton/), in the middle of [Lotus Lake](/world/fanton/lotus-lake-city/lotus-lake/). Is the birthplace and institution of water-style martial arts.
